San Vicente Ferrer Parish Taguig City


Our Church San Vicente Ferrer Parish is a sacred space rooted in faith and tradition. Named after Saint Vincent Ferrer, the patron saint of builders and preachers, our church stands as a beacon of hope and community in Taguig, Philippines.


We are dedicated to fostering a welcoming environment where parishioners can grow spiritually, celebrate the sacraments, and partake in meaningful worship. Located in Palar Village, Barangay Pinagsama, our church is a place of gathering, reflection, and service, committed to sharing the love of Christ with all who enter.

Our mission is to inspire faith, serve others, and build a strong, united community under the guidance and protection of Our Lord and the intercession of Saint Vincent Ferrer.
